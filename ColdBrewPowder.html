Cold Brew Powder

  • Grind 100 g coffee coarsely and mix it with 1 l water, then stir well.
  • Cover your pot and leave the coffee in the fridge for at least 12 hours.
  • Filter the cold-extracted coffee
  • Enjoy the finished Cold Brew pure or pimp it with fruit juices!
